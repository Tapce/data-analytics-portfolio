# 01 — Supply Chain OTIF & Stock Accuracy Analysis

End-to-end analysis of supplier on-time-in-full (OTIF) performance and inventory accuracy across a multi-supplier distribution operation.

## The Business Question

> *Why are we missing OTIF targets, and where should we focus to recover the most ground with the least effort?*

This is the kind of question I worked through repeatedly during my time as a Supply Chain Analyst at NERVI Enterprises and Business Operations Analyst at F.Costa & Son. This project recreates that workflow against a public/anonymised dataset.

## Approach

1. **Data exploration** — understanding what we have, what's missing, and what's trustworthy.
2. **KPI definition** — locking down OTIF, stock accuracy, and supplier lead-time definitions before analysis.
3. **SQL analysis** — variance investigation, supplier ranking, root-cause segmentation.
4. **Dashboard** — Power BI view designed for weekly operational review.
5. **Recommendations** — what we'd do differently on Monday morning.
