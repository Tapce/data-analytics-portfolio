# 02 — Merchant Performance & Forecasting

Replicates the kind of merchant analytics work I led at IMB Financial Services — automating weekly performance reviews, identifying trends, and building a forecasting model that links operational drivers to financial outcomes.

## The Business Question

> *Which merchants are trending toward problems before the numbers say so, and how confident can we be in next quarter's revenue forecast?*

## Approach

1. **Define the merchant performance scorecard** — transaction volume, average transaction value, decline rate, fee yield.
2. **Trend & anomaly detection** — surface merchants whose recent behaviour diverges from baseline.
3. **Forecasting model** — link operational drivers (volume, seasonality, merchant cohort age) to revenue outcomes.
4. **Executive view** — a Tableau dashboard pitched at senior stakeholders, not analysts.
